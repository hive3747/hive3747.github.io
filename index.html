About

We are a FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) highschool robotics team located in Sandy, Utah. We come from Beehive Science and Technology Academy.

FTC is a robotics competition which FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) hosts. The mission of FIRST is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communcation, and leadership.

Our team was originally founded in 2009, and our school has been striving to not only have a great team, but to inspire and educate others about FIRST since.
